Lamborghini

Lamborghini car price starts at Rs 3.10 Crore for the cheapest model which is Urus and the price of most expensive model, which is Huracan STO starts at Rs 4.99 Crore. Lamborghini offers 4 car models in India, including 1 car in SUV category, 2 cars in Coupe category, 1 car in Convertible category. Lamborghini has 1 upcoming car in India, Urus Facelift.


Italian marquee Lamborghini was founded back in 1963. The company has its headquarters located at Sant''Agata Bolognese in Italy and is owned by Volkswagen AG. The brand was founded by Ferrucio Lamborghini.


Currently, Lamborghini sells three models across the globe including the Huracan, Aventador and the Urus range. The company also produces limited edition models such as the Sian and the Centenario. The brand has three showrooms in India located in Mumbai, Delhi and Bengaluru.


Lamborghini unveiled its first ever hybrid vehicle, the Sian, at the 2019 Frankfurt Motor Show. Only 63 units of the model will be produced and all of them have been accounted for. The company is also working on a fully-electric product, details of which are unavailable at the moment.

Lamborghini Urus

Lamborghini takes a giant leap ahead and changes the way we look at SUVs with the launch of the Urus. Named after a species of wild Ox that predates the modern domestic cattle, the Urus is Lamborghini’s second SUV that comes nearly 25 years after their first SUV, the LM002, went out of production. It is based on the same MLB Evo platform as the Porsche Cayenne, Bentley Bentayga and the Audi Q7 under the VW Group umbrella.
