Ford

Ford car price starts at Rs 5.82 Lakh for the cheapest model which is Figo and the price of most expensive model, which is Endeavour starts at Rs 33.81 Lakh. Ford offers 5 car models in India, including 1 car in SUV category, 2 cars in Hatchback category, 1 car in Compact Sedan category, 1 car in Compact SUV category. Ford has 1 upcoming car in India, EcoSport Facelift.


Ford India is a subsidiary of Ford Motor Company, Michigan, U.S.A. The Indian arm of the American automobile giant has its head office in Chennai. The company has been working as an independent arm of the parent company in India since 1998.


Currently, Ford product portfolio in India includes the Figo, Aspire, Freestyle, EcoSport, Endeavour, and the Mustang. The EcoSport was one of the first compact SUV’s to be launched in India. All products save for the Mustang are produced at Ford India’s manufacturing facility located in Chengalpattu, Chennai, and Sanand, Gujarat.


In April 2019, Ford Motor Company partnered with Mahindra and Mahindra to develop an all-new mid-size SUV for developing markets. Apart from an all-new model, both the companies will also be working on mutual areas of interest such as electrification, production development, and production distribution. Apart from local sales, Ford India also exports various products from the country across the globe.

Ford EcoSport

The new Ford EcoSport has been launched with more features over its predecessor. Ford EcoSport is available in six trims – Ambiente, Trend, Titanium, Titanium+, EcoSport S and the Thunder Edition. The newly introduced feature-loaded Thunder Edition is based on the Titanium variant and it replaces the Signature trim.

Ford Endeavour

The big SUV, literally in every sense, the Ford Endeavour comes with fresh cosmetic details and more features. This time around the company has excluded the Trend variant and the SUV is now available only in Titanium and Titanium+ variants. Along with the new features, the SUV gets a manual option for the 2.2-litre engine with a 4x2 configuration.

Ford Figo

The current iteration of the Ford Figo in India had last received an update in 2019. It was a mid-life update for the B-segment hatchback after being on sale for almost four years. The hatchback received subtle cosmetic changes along with rejigged trim levels. Currently, the Ford Figo is available in three variant options – Ambiente, Titanium, and Titanium Blu.

Ford Freestyle

The Ford Freestyle is a crossover hatchback with 190mm ground clearance and SUV-like styling elements. The brand offers the Freestyle in six variants and two engine options which now meet the BS6 emission norms. Ford launched a special edition model of the car, named Freestyle Flair in late 2020. Its price ranges from Rs. 7.09 lakh to 8.84 lakh (ex-showroom).

Ford Aspire

Ford India launched the Aspire sub-four metre sedan in India back in 2018. The model is available with two powertrain options and five colours. The latter includes White Gold, Ruby Red, Moondust Silver, Smoke Grey, and Diamond White.
